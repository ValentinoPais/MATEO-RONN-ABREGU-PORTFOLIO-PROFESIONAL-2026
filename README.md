### Mateo Ronn — Photographer Portfolio

A portfolio website for professional photographer **Mateo Ronn**, designed and built so the
**client manages his own content**: galleries are pulled live from **Google Drive through the
Drive API**, so Mateo adds, removes and reorders photos himself — no developer needed.

> [Live site](https://mateoronnabregu.netlify.app/)

https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/10851a74-5016-405d-873d-7cca40514b75

#### What it solves
A photographer's portfolio changes constantly. Instead of hardcoding images (and being called every
time something changes), the site reads directly from the client's Google Drive — drop a photo into a
folder and it shows up on the site automatically.

#### Highlights
- **Self-managed content** — galleries sync from Google Drive via the Drive API: no redeploys, no dependency on the developer.
- **Clean, image-first design** — built to put the photography front and center.
- **Responsive** — works across desktop and mobile.
- Hosted on Netlify.

#### Stack
`JavaScript` · `HTML` · `CSS` · `Google Drive API` · `Netlify`
